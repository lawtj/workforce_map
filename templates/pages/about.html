Who We Are

We are a collaboration of academic groups and international federations of societies focused on global health. Our initiative brings together surgeons and anesthesiologists to provide a comprehensive view of the global perioperative workforce.

Our Mission

We created this website to address the crucial need for workforce mapping in resolving the surgical and anesthesia workforce deficit. By consolidating data from various groups into one accessible platform, we aim to streamline information retrieval and support health workforce advocacy.

Data and Contributions

The data on this site is administrated by individual contributing groups. For updates, please contact the respective contributors or use our contact page.

This platform is designed to enhance collaboration and understanding among healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers, facilitating informed decision-making in global health.
